## Patterns to watch

### Spanish bleed (pronunciation/vocabulary)

### Grammar patterns
- Subject-verb agreement: singular subjects need singular verbs ("problem was", not "was")
- Tense consistency: past tense in narratives (project descriptions use "did", not present "does")

### Word order
- Adverbs: "also used" (adverb after verb) is more natural than "used also"
